Service FAQs

What's the minimum income requirement?

No minimum income required. We assess your ability to repay based on your overall situation, business potential, or project viability. Self-employed and informal sector workers are welcome.

Do you require collateral?

Most personal and business loans require no collateral. For larger group loans, we may require Group Saving accounts or guarantor agreements as an alternative.

How long does approval take?

Most applications are approved within 24-48 hours. Urgent cases may be reviewed within 4-6 hours. Once approved, funds are disbursed the same day.

Can I repay early without penalties?

Yes! Early repayment is encouraged and comes with no penalties. You'll enjoy interest savings if you pay off your loan ahead of schedule.

What if I miss a payment?

We understand life happens. Contact us immediately. We offer flexible reschedule options to help you get back on track without harming your credit history.

Do you provide financial guidance?

Absolutely! All clients receive free financial literacy training, business planning assistance, and ongoing support through our mobile app and support team.
