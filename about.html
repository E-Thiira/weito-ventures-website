Who We Are

Weito Ventures is a Kenyan microfinance institution dedicated to empowering small businesses and entrepreneurs across the country. Since our founding in 2024, we've been committed to providing accessible, transparent financial solutions that help underserved communities build sustainable livelihoods and achieve economic independence.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide accessible, transparent, and flexible financial solutions that empower underserved communities.

Our Vision

A financially inclusive society where every entrepreneur has the opportunity to thrive.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We operate with honesty and transparency in all our dealings, building trust through clear communication and ethical practices.

Community Focus

We're dedicated to supporting local entrepreneurs and strengthening communities across Kenya through accessible finance.

Innovation

We continuously improve our services and adopt new technologies to better serve our clients' evolving needs.

Empowerment

We believe in giving people the tools and resources they need to achieve financial independence and business success.
